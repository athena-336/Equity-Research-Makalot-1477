# Equity Research: Makalot Industrial (1477.TW) | 聚陽實業產業研究報告

This project provides a comprehensive investment analysis of Makalot Industrial, a leading global apparel manufacturer. The report covers the entire textile value chain—from petrochemical raw materials to retail—and evaluates Makalot’s strategic positioning in the "Fast Fashion" and "Athleisure" segments.
本專案對全球領先的成衣製造商——聚陽實業（1477.TW）進行了深入的投資分析。報告涵蓋了從上游石化原料到下游零售的完整紡織價值鏈，並評估了聚陽在「快時尚」與「機能運動風」趨勢下的戰略定位。

> **政大金融投資與產業研究社 · FIIRG Final Project · 第三組**

---

## 🎯 Objective | 分析目標
To evaluate Makalot's financial health and growth potential within the global apparel industry. The analysis focuses on how Makalot mitigates risks (e.g., inventory gluts, raw material fluctuations) and leverages its Southeast Asian production bases to maintain a competitive moat.
評估聚陽在全成衣產業中的財務健康狀況與成長潛力。分析重點在於聚陽如何應對風險（如庫存去化、原物料波動），並利用其東南亞生產基地建立競爭護城河。

---

## 🛠 Pipeline | 分析流程
1. **Industry Overview:** Mapping the textile supply chain from Upstream (PTA/EG) to Downstream (Apparel). / 產業介紹：梳理從上游石化原料（PTA/EG）到下游成衣的紡織產業鏈。
2. **Company Analysis:** Examining Makalot's "Multi-country, Multi-factory" strategy and its client portfolio (Gap, Target, H&M). / 公司介紹：檢視聚陽的「多國、多廠」佈局及核心客戶結構。
3. **Growth Drivers:** Evaluating the shift towards high-margin functional clothing and sustainable textiles. / 成長動能：評估高毛利機能服飾與永續紡織品的轉型趨勢。
4. **Financial Analysis:** Reviewing revenue trends, profitability ratios, and inventory turnover. / 財務分析：分析營收趨勢、獲利能力指標及庫存週轉率。
5. **Investment Conclusion:** Summarizing strengths, weaknesses, and potential investment risks. / 結論：總結優劣勢與潛在投資風險。

---

## 💻 Tech Stack & Tools | 技術與工具
* **Financial Modeling:** Revenue Forecasting, Profitability Margin Analysis
* **Strategic Frameworks:** SWOT Analysis, Supply Chain Mapping
* **Data Sources:** TEJ (Taiwan Economic Journal), Company Annual Reports, Financial Dog (財報狗)

---

## 🧠 Key Findings | 核心洞察
* **Supply Chain Mastery:** Makalot sits at the downstream of the textile industry, where labor-intensive garment manufacturing benefits from Southeast Asian regional layouts to lower costs. / 供應鏈優勢：聚陽位於產業鏈下游，透過東南亞生產基地的勞動力優勢有效降低生產成本。

* **Client Diversification:** By serving both brand owners and mass-market retailers, Makalot balances its exposure across different consumer segments. / 客戶多元化：透過橫跨品牌商與通路商的客戶群，平衡了不同消費市場的風險。
* **Growth Potential:** Strong performance in Q2 and the expansion of functional clothing production are expected to drive significant year-over-year profit growth. / 成長潛力：預期 Q2 營收將恢復成長，且機能服佔比提升將帶動強勁的獲利能力。
